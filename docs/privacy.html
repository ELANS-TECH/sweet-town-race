🍭 Sweet Town Race — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 July 2026
The short version: Sweet Town Race does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no servers. Everything the app creates stays on your device.

Information we collect

None. Sweet Town Race does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising identifiers, or third-party SDKs that collect data. The app has no login and no user accounts.

Photos and the camera

The game includes an optional feature that lets a player put a photo of themselves (and one passenger) in the car so they appear to be driving. If you use it:

  • The photo is taken or chosen on your device with your permission.
  • It is cropped and stored only on your device.
  • It is never uploaded, transmitted, backed up to us, or shared with anyone.
  • We have no server and no ability to access it.
  • You can remove it at any time with the “Remove” button on the photo screen. Deleting the app also deletes it.

The feature is entirely optional. If you decline camera or photo permission, the game works exactly the same with a friendly cartoon driver instead.

Game progress

Stars, collected treats, accessories, and scrapbook postcards are saved locally on your device so progress isn’t lost between sessions. This information never leaves the device and is removed if you delete the app.

Children’s privacy

Sweet Town Race is designed to be suitable for young children. Because the app collects no personal information at all, there is nothing to disclose, sell, or share, and no data is collected from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your region). The app contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, no social features, no chat, and no external links inside the gameplay.

Third parties

We do not share information with third parties, because we do not collect any.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
