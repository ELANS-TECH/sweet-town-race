How to play

  • Tap the car on the home screen to change its colour — it says the colour out loud.
  • Pick a speed: 🐢 Easy, 🚗 Medium, or 🚀 Hard.
  • Press PLAY and choose a store to drive to.
  • Hold the left or right side of the screen to steer.
  • Drive through candies to collect them, and over yellow ramps to jump.
  • There is no way to lose — bumps only slow you down for a moment.
  • When you reach the store, tap the falling treats to catch them.

Frequently asked questions

Can my child lose or fail? No. There are no fail states, timers, or game-overs. Obstacles cause a small wobble and nothing more.

What is the photo feature? Your child can optionally put their photo in the car so they appear to be driving, plus a passenger. Photos stay on the device and are never uploaded. Tap 📸 then “Remove 🗑” to delete a photo at any time. The feature is optional — the game works normally without it.

Are there ads or purchases? No. No advertising, no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, no chat, and no links out of the game.

How do I turn off the sound? Tap the 🔊 button in the top-right corner.

What is the basket for? Treats collected in races are spent in the 🔧 Garage on hats, sunglasses, balloons, stripes, and stickers. Each item needs particular treats — so it pays to visit every store, vegetables included!

My progress disappeared. Progress is stored on the device. Deleting and reinstalling the app, or clearing its data, will reset it.
